## üé≠ 2. Generating with Presets
Perfect for quick, high-quality narrations.

1.  **Select a Voice:** Choose from the list of handcrafted presets (e.g., "Raspy Wizard", "Friendly News") in the right panel.
2.  **Enter Text:** Type your script into the main text box.
3.  **Adjust Language:** Select your target language from the sidebar (or use "Auto" for automatic detection).
4.  **Generate:** Click **"Generate Audio"**. Your generation will appear in the "Library" below.

---

## ‚ú® 3. Designing a Voice
Create a brand new voice from a text description.

1.  **Switch to "Voice Design" tab.**
2.  **Describe the Voice:** Use natural language (e.g., *"A deep, soothing male voice with a slight British accent and a touch of gravel"*).
3.  **Generate:** The AI will interpret your prompt and synthesize the voice on the fly.
4.  **Save:** If you like it, you can save it as a custom preset.

---

## üé§ 4. Cloning a Voice
Synthesize speech that sounds exactly like a specific person.

1.  **Switch to "Voice Clone" tab.**
2.  **Upload Audio:** Upload a clean 10-30 second clip of the voice you want to clone (WAV or MP3).
3.  **Reference Text:** Type what the person in the audio is saying (optional, but improves accuracy).
4.  **Generate:** Input your new script and click **"Generate"**.

---

## üí° Pro Tips
- **Model Size:** Use **1.7B** for the best quality, or **0.6B** for blazing speed.
- **Language Auto-Detection:** You can leave the language on "Auto", and VoiceForge will detect it from your text.
- **Short Clips:** For cloning, high-quality audio with no background noise works best.
- **Library Management:** Use folders in the Library section to organize your generations!
